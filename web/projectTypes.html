Our Types of Projects

Residental projects

Whether it is renovations on your house, or even building one from scratch we have you covered.

General budget range: $100,000-$500,000

Timeline: from 6 to 12 months

Commercial projects

From building smaller restaurants to large skyscrapers for office space we have experience with construction of commercial buildings with a wide range of uses.

General budget range: $500,000 to $2,000,000

Timeline: from 12 to 24 months

Industrial projects

If your company is in need of new industrial facilities like manufacturing plants, warehouses or perhaps distribution centers we can proudly say that we have plenty of experience in the field.

General budget range: $2,000,000 to $10,000,000

Timeline: from 24 to 36 months

Road construction

If your buildings and facilities aren't yet connected with roads we can solve that problem for you. We have experience with construction of both highways and regular roads, bridges and tunnels included.

General budget range: $1,000,000 to $5,000,000

Timeline: from 12 to 24 months
